01 // THE PROCESS

From Target to Insight

Four phases of social engineering analysis. From reconnaissance to detection. The all-seeing eye misses nothing.

01
RECON
OSINT profiling across public sources. Map digital footprints, social graphs, and attack surfaces.
02
CRAFT
Build phishing templates, pretexting scripts, and influence payloads. AI-assisted scenario generation.
03
SIMULATE
Deploy controlled phishing campaigns. Track opens, clicks, and credential submissions in real-time.
04
ANALYZE
Email forensics with SPF/DKIM/DMARC analysis. Detect influence techniques and manipulation patterns.
02 // FEATURES

The Panopticon Arsenal

Every tool needed to understand, simulate, and defend against social engineering. Knowledge is the ultimate defense.

PHISHING SIMULATOR
Deploy realistic phishing campaigns for security testing. Track click rates, credential captures, and user awareness metrics.
EMAIL FORENSICS
Deep header analysis with SPF, DKIM, DMARC validation. Trace email origins and detect spoofing attempts.
OSINT PROFILING
Automated reconnaissance across public data. Build target profiles from social media, DNS records, and breached data.
PRETEXTING ENGINE
Generate convincing pretexting scenarios. Role-play based social engineering with AI-powered conversation trees.
INFLUENCE DETECTION
Identify manipulation techniques in communications. Detect urgency, authority abuse, scarcity tactics, and social proof exploits.
CREDENTIAL HARVESTING
Controlled credential capture pages for authorized penetration testing. Full audit trail and encrypted storage.
CAMPAIGN ANALYTICS
Real-time dashboards for active campaigns. Click-through rates, time-to-click, department vulnerability scoring.
CERBERUS INTEGRATION
Part of the greater security suite. Feeds intelligence into the CERBERUS threat correlation engine.
